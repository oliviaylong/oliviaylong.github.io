I've decided to take on a very important, very unfunded research project: finding the best coffee shop in Berkeley.

The methodology is still a work in progress, but so far my criteria are:
- **Coffee drink quality** — I am not elite enough to drink my coffee black, so I will be judging based on the taste and creativity of the drinks
- **Seating** — a real chair, a real table, and enough room to open a laptop without elbowing a stranger
- **Noise level** — quiet enough to focus, lively enough not to feel like a library
- **Vibe** — hard to define, easy to recognize

** to be updated!! stay tuned **

This is very much an ongoing quest -- a slice of my life!
