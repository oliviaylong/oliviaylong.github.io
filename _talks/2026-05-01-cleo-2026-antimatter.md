Olivia Y. Long, Peter B. Catrysse, Seunghoon Han, and Shanhui Fan. We demonstrate that passive, lossy materials with positive index at real frequencies can be used to realize ideal optical antimatter, through engineering of complex electromagnetic parameters under specific frequency conditions.
